# Prompt 2: UGC Campaign Brief

## System Context
You are an expert in user-generated content strategy, organic social amplification, and DTC brand marketing. You know the difference between earned UGC, paid UGC, and creator-seeded UGC — and how to structure campaigns that generate authentic content at scale. You understand how brands like GoPro, Glossier, Duolingo, and Notion built content engines from customer content.

### SECTION 4: ORGANIC SEEDING STRATEGY

**How to generate UGC without paying for it:**

1. **Packaging Insert** 
   > "Love [PRODUCT NAME]? Share your experience on [PLATFORM] with #[HASHTAG] for a chance to be featured on our page. Best posts win [PRIZE/DISCOUNT/FEATURE]."

2. **Post-Purchase Email** (send Day 7 after delivery)
   > Subject: "We'd love to see your [PRODUCT NAME] in action"
   > Body: 2-3 sentences + clear CTA + hashtag + prize incentive

3. **Community Challenge** (if applicable)
   > Challenge name: #[BRAND][ACTION]Challenge
   > Mechanic: [Simple action that creates shareable content]
   > Prize: [Feature on brand page + gift card]

4. **Review-to-Content Pipeline**
   > 5-star reviewers → DM asking to turn their review into a video → offer upgrade/discount as incentive

### SECTION 5: REPURPOSING PLAN

| UGC Asset | Where to Repurpose |
|-----------|-------------------|
| TikTok/Reels video | Meta paid ads (Advantage+ creative), YouTube pre-roll |
| Still images | Product page, email header, Pinterest, Google Shopping |
| Quote from caption | Website testimonial widget, social proof overlay in ads |
| B-roll footage | Website hero section loop, email GIF |
| Before/after | Before/after comparison slider on product page |

**Paid Ads Repurposing — Key Rules:**
- Always confirm usage rights cover paid advertising before boosting
- Use as-is (don't over-edit — authenticity is why it converts)
- Test 3-5 hooks as separate ad variants
- Best performing organic UGC → DPA (Dynamic Product Ad) creative testing
