# Prompt 1: Influencer Discovery Brief + Outreach Email

## System Context
You are an expert influencer marketing strategist with 10 years of experience running campaigns for DTC brands, SaaS companies, and consumer apps. You understand FTC guidelines, engagement benchmarks by tier and platform, and what makes an outreach email actually get a response.

## User Prompt Template

```
Generate a complete influencer discovery brief and outreach kit for:

BRAND: [Brand name]
PRODUCT/SERVICE: [What you're selling]
TARGET AUDIENCE: [Who buys it — demographics, interests, pain points]
CAMPAIGN GOAL: [Brand awareness / Sales / App installs / UGC content / Event attendance]
BUDGET TIER: [NANO ($10-$100/post) / MICRO ($100-$1K/post) / MACRO ($1K-$10K/post) / MEGA ($10K+/post)]
PRIMARY PLATFORM: [Instagram / TikTok / YouTube / LinkedIn / Pinterest / Twitch]
NICHE: [Beauty / Fitness / Tech / Food / Finance / Parenting / Gaming / Travel / Fashion / etc.]
CAMPAIGN TIMELINE: [Start date → End date]
UNIQUE VALUE PROP: [1 sentence — what makes your product stand out]
```

**Red Flags — Auto-Disqualify:**
- [ ] Engagement rate below threshold
- [ ] Follower/following ratio > 10:1 (purchased followers signal)
- [ ] Previous brand deal with direct competitor (check grid + tagged posts)
- [ ] Comments are generic/emoji-only (bot engagement)
- [ ] Audience demographics mismatch > 40%
- [ ] No FTC disclosure on previous sponsored posts
- [ ] Content quality inconsistent with brand aesthetic

**Green Flags — Prioritize:**
- [ ] Saves/shares rate > 2% (signals high-value content)
- [ ] Audience comments ask buying questions
- [ ] Creator has promoted similar products organically
- [ ] Story views > 30% of follower count
- [ ] Response rate documented by agency/platform

**Approved Disclosure Language:**
- "Ad" or "#ad" — always acceptable
- "Paid partnership with [BRAND]" — Instagram/Facebook native tag
- "This video is sponsored by [BRAND]" — YouTube (verbal + text card)
- "#gifted" — if product received free; "#gifted #ad" if also compensated
- What's NOT compliant: #spon, #collab, buried in long hashtag string, only in bio

**Creator Brief Language to Include:**
> "Per FTC guidelines, you must clearly disclose this partnership at the beginning of your caption, 
> video description, or verbal acknowledgment in video. Use #ad, #sponsored, or the Instagram/TikTok 
> paid partnership tag. Non-compliant posts will require immediate revision."
