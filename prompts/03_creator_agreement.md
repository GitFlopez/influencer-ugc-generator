# Prompt 3: Creator Collaboration Agreement Outline

## System Context
You are an influencer marketing legal and operations expert. You've drafted hundreds of creator agreements for brands ranging from Shopify stores to Fortune 500 companies. You know what terms protect brands, what terms creators will push back on, and how to structure deals that feel fair to creators while protecting the brand's IP and compliance obligations.

Note: This is a document outline for reference. Have a licensed attorney review before use in high-stakes campaigns.

## User Prompt Template

```
Generate a complete creator collaboration agreement outline and campaign brief for:

BRAND: [Brand name]
CREATOR TIER: [GIFTED (free product only) / PAID (flat fee) / AFFILIATE (commission only) / HYBRID (gifted + affiliate) / AMBASSADOR (ongoing retainer)]
DELIVERABLES: [e.g., "2 TikTok videos, 3 Instagram Stories, 1 Instagram Reel"]
EXCLUSIVITY: [Yes/No. If yes: duration (30/60/90/180 days) and category (e.g., "beauty brands only")]
USAGE RIGHTS SCOPE: [Organic social / Paid social ads / Website / Email / Out-of-home / All]
USAGE RIGHTS DURATION: [6 months / 12 months / 2 years / In perpetuity]
COMPENSATION: [$X flat / $X + $Y bonus at X threshold / X% affiliate / Gifted product (value $X)]
CAMPAIGN DATES: [Go-live date range]
REVISION ROUNDS: [How many rounds of feedback before additional fees]
```

### SECTION 4: FTC & PLATFORM COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST

**Before Publishing — Creator Must:**

**Instagram:**
- [ ] Use "Paid partnership with [Brand]" label (native tag) OR #ad in first line of caption
- [ ] Stories: text overlay "#ad" visible for full 3-second view time
- [ ] Do NOT bury disclosure in long hashtag string

**TikTok:**
- [ ] Enable "Branded content" toggle in TikTok (auto-adds disclosure)
- [ ] OR include #ad in caption above the fold

**YouTube:**
- [ ] Verbal disclosure in first 30 seconds: "This video is sponsored by [Brand]"
- [ ] Check "Paid promotion" box in upload settings
- [ ] Text card disclosure in first 30 seconds

**Blog/Website:**
- [ ] Disclosure at top of post, before affiliate links
- [ ] "I received [product] for free in exchange for this review" if applicable

**What Happens if Creator Doesn't Comply:**
1. Brand will request immediate revision
2. Creator must update disclosure within 24 hours
3. Failure to comply: brand reserves right to withhold final payment
4. FTC fines are creator's liability if non-disclosure is willful

---

### SECTION 5: EXCLUSIVITY + NON-DISPARAGEMENT

**Exclusivity Clause:**
> "During the exclusivity period of [X days] following content go-live, Creator agrees not to promote, review, or create sponsored content for any [direct competitor / brand in same category] as defined as: [specific category definition, e.g., 'protein supplement brands', 'project management SaaS tools']. Organic non-sponsored mentions of competitors do not violate this clause."

**Non-Disparagement:**
> "Creator agrees not to publicly disparage, defame, or make materially false statements about [BRAND NAME], its products, employees, or affiliates during the contract term and for [12 months] following termination."

**What's NOT restricted:**
- Honest reviews of competitor products that are not sponsored
- Responding to audience questions about competitors
- Sharing personal opinions in non-sponsored content

### SECTION 6: PAYMENT TERMS + MILESTONE SCHEDULE

| Milestone | Amount | Trigger | Payment Method |
|-----------|--------|---------|----------------|
| Agreement signed | 50% | Contract execution | [PayPal / Wise / ACH] |
| Content approved | 50% | Brand written approval | Within 5 business days |
| Performance bonus (if applicable) | Bonus amount | 14 days post-live | End of month |

**Late Payment:** [X]% interest per month on overdue balance after [30] days.

**Cancellation by Brand:** 
- Before content creation begins: 25% kill fee
- After draft submitted: 50% kill fee  
- After content approved: 100% of fee (content delivered)

**Cancellation by Creator:**
- Before content creation begins: No penalty (return any gifted product if requested)
- After draft submitted: Must deliver or return 50% of any advance
- After approval: Full refund required if post not published

---

### SECTION 7: WHAT TO DO IF CREATOR POSTS NON-COMPLIANT CONTENT

**Step 1:** DM or email within 24 hours with specific correction required
**Step 2:** If no response in 48 hours, escalate to manager/agent with written notice
**Step 3:** If still no resolution in 72 hours, withhold final payment and document everything
**Step 4:** If FTC violation (missing disclosure on paid post), send formal written notice citing CFR § 255.5
**Step 5:** If defamatory/false claim posted: issue cease-and-desist via attorney

**Documentation to Keep:**
- Signed agreement or DM confirmation of terms
- Screenshot of all posts at time of publication
- All communication (keep email thread)
- Screenshots of any non-compliant posts before deletion
