# Marketing Strategy — Influencer & UGC Campaign Generator

## Marketplace Listing Copy

**Headline:** Generate complete influencer campaigns in 20 minutes — outreach, briefs, agreements, and ROI reports

**Subheadline:** 4 prompts that cover the full lifecycle: discovery to reporting. No agency needed.

**Description (400 words):**

Running an influencer campaign sounds simple until you're 3 hours deep in a Google Doc trying to figure out what to send an influencer, what to put in the content brief, what rights you actually own, and whether your ROAS was any good.

This skill handles all of it.

**Prompt 1: Influencer Discovery Brief + Outreach**
Get a complete influencer criteria scorecard (engagement rate thresholds, audience demographics, red flags) plus three outreach email formats — a 300-character TikTok/Instagram DM for nano/micro creators, a full email pitch for macro influencers, and a media kit request for mega/celebrity talent. Includes 5 subject line variants and a 2-touch follow-up sequence. FTC disclosure requirements included for every platform.

**Prompt 2: UGC Campaign Brief**
Generate three creative concepts for your campaign, a detailed content brief (what to film, messaging dos and don'ts, hashtags, caption format), a paid UGC creator talent brief with compensation benchmarks, an organic seeding strategy (packaging inserts, post-purchase emails, challenge mechanics), a repurposing plan for ads/website/email, and legal rights capture language.

**Prompt 3: Creator Agreement Outline**
Build a full collaboration brief (deliverables table, approval process, revision rounds), compensation structure options (flat fee / gifted+affiliate / retainer), a usage rights matrix with platform-specific duration and pricing guidance, FTC/ASA compliance checklist for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and blogs, exclusivity and non-disparagement clause language, payment milestones, and what to do if a creator posts non-compliant content.

**Prompt 4: Campaign Performance Report**
Calculate CPM, CPE, CPC, CPA, ROAS, and Earned Media Value (EMV) — with the formula explained. Get a full creator performance ranking table, top/bottom content analysis, paid vs. organic benchmark comparison, full campaign P&L, and a budget reallocation recommendation for the next campaign.

The whole influencer marketing industry is built around agencies charging $2,000-$10,000 to do exactly this. This skill gives you the same output for $29.
