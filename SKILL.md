## Prompts

### Prompt 1: Influencer Discovery Brief + Outreach Email
**Input:** Brand name, product/service, target audience, campaign goal, budget tier (nano/micro/macro/mega), niche  
**Output:**
- Influencer criteria scorecard (follower range, engagement rate minimum, content pillars, audience demographics, red flags to avoid)
- Platform selection rationale (Instagram vs TikTok vs YouTube vs LinkedIn vs Pinterest)
- 3-tier outreach emails (nano/micro: personalized DM, macro: email pitch, mega/celebrity: media kit request)
- Subject line variants (5 options)
- Follow-up sequence (2-touch: 7 days, 14 days)
- FTC disclosure requirements for each tier

### Prompt 2: UGC Campaign Brief
**Input:** Brand name, product, desired content type (review/unboxing/tutorial/lifestyle/before-after), platform(s), incentive structure, timeline  
**Output:**
- Campaign concept + creative direction (3 concept options)
- UGC content brief (what to film/photograph, messaging guidelines, brand dos & don'ts, hashtags)
- Talent brief for paid UGC creators (deliverables, usage rights requested, compensation benchmarks)
- Organic seeding strategy (how to encourage unpaid UGC)
- Repurposing plan (how to use UGC across ads, website, email, social)
- Legal rights capture: submission form copy + usage rights language

### Prompt 3: Creator Collaboration Agreement Outline
**Input:** Creator tier (paid/gifted/affiliate), deliverables, exclusivity requirements, usage rights scope, compensation type  
**Output:**
- Full collaboration brief document (deliverables, timeline, approval process, revision rounds)
- Compensation structure options (flat fee / performance bonus / affiliate / gifted + commission)
- Usage rights matrix (paid social, organic, website, out-of-home — duration + platform scope)
- FTC/ASA compliance checklist (disclosure language for each platform)
- Non-disparagement + exclusivity clause language
- Payment terms + milestone schedule
- What to do if creator posts non-compliant content

### Prompt 4: Campaign Performance Report Template
**Input:** Campaign name, platform, number of creators, total spend, impressions, engagements, clicks, conversions, revenue generated  
**Output:**
- Executive summary (1-page brief for stakeholders)
- KPI dashboard (CPM, CPE, CPC, CPA, ROAS, EMV — Earned Media Value formula included)
- Creator performance ranking table (top performers + underperformers)
- Content performance analysis (top 3 posts by engagement, top 3 by conversion)
- ROI calculation with comparison to paid social benchmarks
- Learnings + recommendations for next campaign
- Budget reallocation recommendation
